About me

I'm On My Way To Becomming a Certified Yoga Instructor

I like to say I accidentally fell into Yoga after having a bad landing in my Capoeira class. I twisted my ankle after attempting a backflip. After being in multiple fitness disciplines as well as trained under several forms of martial arts; I was desperate to find an alternate outlet of fitness while I was healing. Something to train in without causing further harm. Something low impact but challenging. Some friends suggested I try Yoga. I went to my first ‚Äúofficial ‚Äú class on st.marks place in New York City and It has now been a part of my life for over 15 years. My practice has helped me grow stronger, more flexible and has often been my therapy especially during injury and recovery both on and off the mat. Yoga inspires me to live each moment more aware and mindfully. I completed my 200 hour Yoga teacher training with YogaMu in 2021 and will continue to advance into my 500 hour practice.

I believe Yoga is a way of life. As a daily practitioner I know I will always be a student of the practice and am deeply grateful for the inspiration, wisdom and guidance that my teachers have shared with me. In my classes, I love to combine playful sequencing, a focus on positive intent and a dose of tough love to help you deepen your practice. I seek to help others thrive in Yoga by guiding you to nurture and listen to your body, mind and soul. My goal is that no matter where you are in life, no matter how you feel that you step off the mat feeling refreshed, nourished, balanced and fearless.
